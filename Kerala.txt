<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Backwaters">Backwaters</H2></A>
Wherever and however you choose to explore the backwaters, this emerald watery paradise is a definite highlight of Kerala. Find out more with our Introduction to the Backwaters and best ways to experience the
backwaters guides.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Fort Cochin">Fort Cochin</H2></A>
Wherever and however you choose to explore the Fort Cochin, this emerald watery paradise is a definite highlight of Kerala. Find out more with our Introduction to the Fort Cochin and best ways to experience the
Fort Cochin guides.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Munnar">Munnar</H2></A>
A beautiful, tranquil hill station with a cool climate, plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities and visits to tea plantations. Read our Guide to Munnar, Kerala & Quiet Alternatives to Munnar, Kerala.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary">Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary</H2></A>
A fantastic place to take boat, jeep or hiking trips to spot elephants, monkeys and even the elusive tiger.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Kollam">Kollam</H2></A>
This small market toen is an obvious attraction, but is the southern gateway to the backwaters, and a quieter alternative to Alleppey for organising houseboat and canoe trips. We also enjoyed the untouristy and
authentically Indian feel of the town.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Varkala">Varkala</H2></A>
A gorgeous beach below rocky cliffs lined with chilled out cafes - the perfect place to watch the spectacular sunsets.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Kumarakom">Kumarakom</H2></A>
At this bird sanctuary near Kottayam you can walk or take a boat ride to spot comorants, herons, egrets and storks.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Thrissur">Thrissur</H2></A>
A religious and cultural centre featuring one of the largest temples in Kerala.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Kozhikode">Kozhikode</H2></A>
This historic town is the gateway to the quieter beaches and backwaters of Norhtern Kerala, with temples and musems to visit too.</p></font>
<H2><p align = "left"><font face = "Calibri"><A Name = "Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary">Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary</H2></A>
A quieter alternative to Periyar as a great place to spot elephants elephants in the wild. We also featured it as a Quiet Alternative to Munnar, Kerala as a more peaceful hill station retreat.</p></font>
